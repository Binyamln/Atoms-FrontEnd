About ATOMS

ATOMS revolutionizes hiring by leveraging artificial intelligence to match candidates with job opportunities, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in the recruitment process. Our solution utilizes advanced ML and NLP techniques and is built for scalability using Docker and GPU acceleration.
